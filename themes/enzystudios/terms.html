Terms & Conditions

1. Sessions Booking

Times and dates are subject to availability. ENZY Studios reserve the right to refuse and reschedule. Bookings can be made online or by person at the facility and are not valid until confirmed and deposit is paid.

2. Deposits & Cancellations

A 25% deposit is required to confirm all bookings of the studio and session musicians. The remaining cost will be paid at the end of the recording session. Failure to produce full payment will result in the studio withholding all copies of recordings, master or session files until all fees have been paid and funds cleared. This includes payment due to breakages or missing items. Further failure to pay will result in prosecution.

If the client must cancel a session, we must be notified within 4 days of the scheduled date. In such circumstances, ENZY Studios must receive an email or call within the time frame and the session may be rescheduled. Please note, only one amendment can be made per booking. If a cancellation is made in less than 4 days of the scheduled session date then your deposit will be forfeit. If a client fails to attend a session this will be deemed as a cancellation and the deposit will be forfeit. There will be no refund in case the client leaves the session early.

5. Smoking

Smoking is permitted only in the designated area. Please ensure the doors are closed if you go to smoke in the designated area.

The studio operates a zero tolerance policy on illegal drugs.

7. Food/Drink

You are permitted to bring your own food and drink into the studio common areas. However, food or drink is strictly prohibited in the control and recording rooms. Any damage caused to studio equipment due to spillages and negligence will be charged to the client.

Copyright in all materials generated at a session will reside with ENZY Studios until such time as all costs of hire/ consumables/ breakages/ missing items are paid for in full with cleared funds.
